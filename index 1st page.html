Why Use This Tool?

Food allergies can be life‑threatening. Whether you're shopping, cooking, or dining out, knowing what’s in your food is essential. Our Food Allergen Detector helps you quickly analyze ingredient lists and identify common allergens before you take a bite.

How It Works
